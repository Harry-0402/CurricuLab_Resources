What is Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a digital record book where data is stored in blocks that are linked together and cannot be changed easily. Each block stores information and is shared across many computers, making it very secure.

In other words, blockchain creates trust without needing a middleman like a bank.

Example:
In cryptocurrency like Bitcoin, blockchain records every transaction so no one can cheat or change past records. Similarly, companies use blockchain to track products in supply chains.

Blockchain is important in digital transformation because it improves transparency, security, and trust.
