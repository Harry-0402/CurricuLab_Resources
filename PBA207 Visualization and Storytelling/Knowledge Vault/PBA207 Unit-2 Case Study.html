Retail competition has intensified with aggressive discounting by Target and Costco. Online platforms are also offering flash sales. Walmart introduced three types of promotional campaigns across 300 stores:

  • Flat 10% discount

  • Buy-One-Get-One (BOGO)

  • Digital coupon-based discounts

3. Core Business Problem

3. Core Business Problem

The research problem is:
‚ÄúDoes the Smart Recommendations feature significantly increase viewing time and reduce churn?‚Äù

4. Key Data and Metrics

A 4-week pilot test across 2 million users:

3. Core Business Problem

The research problem:
‚ÄúDoes redesigned store layout significantly increase impulse purchase revenue?‚Äù

4. Key Data and Metrics

Pilot Stores (50):

  • Average impulse revenue increase: 12%

  • Average total sales increase: 5%

Non-pilot stores (100):

  • Impulse revenue increase: 6%

Management must decide whether to implement layout redesign across 1,200 stores nationwide.
